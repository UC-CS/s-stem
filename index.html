UC Computer Science S-STEM Project

Introduction

The demand for computer-science graduates is booming in Ohio, driven by major investments such as Intel's $20 billion chip factories and GE Aerospace's headquarters in Cincinnati. The Southwest Ohio Scholars Program (SOSP) at the University of Cincinnati aims to support 36 low-income computer science students over 5 years. By providing scholarships, academic and social support, and real-world experience, SOSP helps build the next generation of computer scientists and engineers to meet Ohio's growing industry needs.
